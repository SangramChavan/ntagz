THE UNDERLYING TECH

What is Near Field Communication (NFC)?

NFC is a short-range, wireless connectivity standard operating at 13.56 MHz. It enables chips embedded in our smart physical items to securely transmit data to micro-antennas inside modern mobile phones instantly with a simple, close-range tap.
