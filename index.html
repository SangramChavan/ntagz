FAQ

Questions? We have
quick answers.

How do I place an order? +
Simply click Order Now in the menu or on any product card. You'll be taken to our quote calculator where you can select your product, quantity, and get an instant price — then confirm via WhatsApp or UPI.
Do I need an app to use the NFC tags? +
No app needed at all. Any Android phone with NFC (most phones from 2016+) or iPhone XR and above can tap and read the tags natively. Your contact just taps — their browser opens your link instantly.
Can I change what the chip links to later? +
Yes! Standard NTAG chips are re-writable. You can reprogram the tag anytime using any free NFC writing app. Locked chips (for permanent use) are also available on request.
How long does bulk shipping take? +
Blank stock standard orders ship within 24 hours. Custom printed products take 2–3 business days for printing before dispatch. We ship pan-India via Blue Dart and Delhivery. Delivery typically takes 2–4 days.
What's the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +
Our minimum order quantity is just 10 pieces per product — making it easy for small businesses and first-time buyers to get started. Bulk discounts kick in from 500 pcs onwards, going up to 40% off at 5,000+ pcs. Not sure which product to order? Try our Sample Kit — 10 pcs of all 7 products in one box!
Is ntagz available on Amazon, Flipkart or IndiaMart? +
Yes — you'll find ntagz products on Amazon.in, Flipkart and IndiaMART, in addition to ordering directly on this website via WhatsApp. Direct orders get the best bulk pricing, custom branding options, and a dedicated account manager.
