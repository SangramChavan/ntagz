FAQ

Questions? We have
quick answers.

Do I need an app to use the NFC card? +
No app needed at all. Any Android phone with NFC (most phones from 2016+) or iPhone XR and above can tap and read the card natively. Your contact just taps — their browser opens your link instantly.
Can I change what the card links to later? +
Yes! Standard NTAG chips are re-writable. You can reprogram the card anytime using our free ntagz app or any NFC writing app. Locked chips (for permanent use) are also available on request.
Do you provide GST invoice? +
Absolutely. All orders come with proper GST-compliant invoices. Our GSTIN is mentioned on the invoice. Bulk corporate orders can be invoiced with PO reference numbers too.
How long does shipping take? +
Standard orders ship within 24 hours. Custom printed products take 2–3 business days for printing before dispatch. We ship pan-India via Blue Dart and Delhivery. Delivery typically takes 2–4 days.
What's the minimum order for custom printing? +
Minimum 10 cards for custom logo printing on business cards. For stickers and tags, minimum 25 pieces. Custom shapes and special finishes (matte, gloss, metallic) are available from 50 pieces.
Can I resell ntagz products? +
Yes, we have an active reseller and distributor program across India. WhatsApp us with your business details and we'll set you up with reseller pricing, marketing support, and dedicated stock allocation.
